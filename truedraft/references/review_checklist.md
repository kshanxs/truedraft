# Review Checklist

## Purpose

Audit the draft before delivery. This checklist is about honesty and readiness, not fake certainty.

---

## Checklist

- [ ] The introduction is analytical, not definitional
- [ ] The structure matches the approved outline or the deviation is explained
- [ ] Major claims are explained, not merely named
- [ ] Stock examples from the avoid-list are gone
- [ ] Repeated filler and robotic phrasing have been reduced
- [ ] Unsupported claims are still marked
- [ ] Citations were not invented
- [ ] The conclusion adds perspective instead of only repeating earlier points
- [ ] The final trust note matches the active mode
- [ ] Delivery status is honest about any remaining placeholders
